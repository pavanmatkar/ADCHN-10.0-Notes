* Switching protocol

In networking (especially enterprise networks), switches are organized in a 3-layer
 hierarchical model. This helps in better performance, scalability, and 
troubleshooting.

1. core layer switch or layer 3 switch needs to be configured to be used as a router
e.g. 7000, 8000, 10000+ series switch

core layer switch is also known as cut through switch or multilayer switch or top layer switch

2. layer 2 switch aka distribution layer switch e.g. 3000 - 5000 series switch

security and configurations are applied on distribution layer switch

3. access layer switch e.g. 1900 - 2900 series switch
