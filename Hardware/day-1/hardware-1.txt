1. Motherboard

Pci slot me NIC card and motherboard tester laga sakte hai 

Pci slot ke right side me graphics card slot hota hai

North Bridge CPU (processor) and RAM ke bich me jo distance hai iske bich me kaam karta hai

South bridge jitney bhi on board port hai na jispe aap aapne keyboard mouse ya sata 
cable connect kar rahe ho in sab ki connectivity responsibility south bridge ki hoti hai


2. SMPS

AC power input DC power output

Cooling fan or Heat sink is present to avoid heating

Maximum 24 power pin, minimum 6 pin, 8 pin, 12 pin, 20 pin

HDD requires 17V DC power supply
FAN 12V DC
 
Black wire is known as ground matlab ye minus hai
Yellow wire is +12V
Orange wire is +3.3V
Brown is PG
Green is PS-ON
Red is +5V
White is -5V
Blue is -12V

Agar ham power button press kar rahe hai baar baar phir bhi pc start nahi ho raha to first ham smps ko check karenge. Usme green and black wire ko short karenge. Agar hamara smps ka fan running hone laga to smps working hai. Uske baad hame motherboard check karna honga.

Agar galtise dusre wire short ho gaye to smps me laga hua capacitor jor se blast hota hai.
